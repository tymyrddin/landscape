Active defence#

What?#

Active defence can use limited offensive action and counterattacks with a defensive posture to deny a contested area or position to an adversary. It requires proactive, anticipatory, and reactionary actions against adversaries. The enemies are already inside the gates …

Why?#

Passive defence typically uses traditional static defenses and then hopes for the best … Adding active defence we can perhaps slow down adversaries and make hacking our devices and (small) networks more difficult and costly.
